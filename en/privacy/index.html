01Data Controller

WTRH, Switzerland — info@swissfuelfinder.com
The Operator is the controller responsible for processing your personal data under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the GDPR.

02Data Collection and Processing

We collect only the data necessary to provide the services of SFF:

  • Authentication Data: Email address and Google ID (processed via Google Firebase).
  • User-Provided Profiles: Pseudo, vehicle tank size, and average consumption. Note: The "Pseudo" cannot be modified once registered.
  • Precise Location Data: GPS coordinates used only while the app is in the foreground to identify nearby stations and calculate routes. Not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Community Contributions: If you update a price, your User ID is linked to that update to prevent fraud.
03Legal Basis for Processing
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR / Art. 31(2)(a) nFADP): To provide the app functions you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: To prevent fraud and improve app performance.
  • Consent: For personalized advertising (AdMob) and precise location tracking.
04Third-Party Services & International Data Transfer

Your data may be transferred outside Switzerland/EEA, notably to the USA.

  • Google Cloud / Firebase: Infrastructure and database hosting.
  • Google AdMob: Ad delivery. Opt-out available in device settings.
  • Payrexx / TWINT: Payment processing. We do not store card or bank details.
  • Safeguards: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the Swiss FDPIC.
05Data Retention
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Technical Logs: Deleted after 30 days.
  • Legal Obligations: Payment data stored for 10 years (Swiss Code of Obligations).
06Your Rights
  • Access: Request details on your stored data.
  • Correction: Update your profile (Pseudo cannot be changed — exercise Erasure right to reset).
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for marketing purposes.
07Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art technical and organizational measures (HTTPS, encryption) to protect your data.
