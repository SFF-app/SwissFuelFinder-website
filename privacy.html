01Data Controller

WTRH, Switzerland — info@swissfuelfinder.com
The Operator is the controller responsible for the processing of your personal data under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the GDPR.

02Data Collection and Processing

We collect only the data necessary to provide the services of SFF:

  • Authentication Data: Email address and Google ID (processed via Google Firebase).
  • User-Provided Profiles: Pseudo, vehicle tank size, and average consumption. Note: For reasons of technical integrity and security, the "Pseudo" cannot be modified once registered.
  • Precise Location Data: GPS coordinates are used only while the app is in the foreground to identify nearby stations and calculate routes. Precise coordinates are processed locally on your device and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Community Contributions: If you update a price, your User ID is linked to that update to prevent fraud and maintain data integrity.
03Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR / Art. 31(2)(a) nFADP): To provide the app functions you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: To prevent fraud and improve app performance.
  • Consent: Specifically for personalized advertising (AdMob) and precise location tracking.
04Third-Party Services & International Data Transfer

To provide SFF, we use third-party providers. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of Switzerland/EEA, notably the USA.

  • Google Cloud / Firebase: For infrastructure and database hosting.
  • Google AdMob: For ad delivery. You may opt-out of personalized tracking in your device settings.
  • Payrexx / TWINT: For payment processing. We do not store your credit card or bank details.
  • Safeguards: For transfers to the USA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).
05Data Retention
  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Technical Logs: Deleted after 30 days.
  • Legal Obligations: Payment-related data is stored for 10 years as required by the Swiss Code of Obligations.
06Your Rights

Under Swiss and EU law, you have the right to:

  • Information & Access: Request details on your stored data.
  • Correction: Update your vehicle profile and settings at any time. Please note that the Pseudo (Username) cannot be modified once chosen. If you wish to change your Pseudo, you must exercise your Right to Erasure to delete your account and create a new one.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for marketing purposes.
07Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art technical and organizational measures (HTTPS, encryption) to protect your data.
